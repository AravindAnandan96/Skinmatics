A science-first approach to
clearer, smoother skin

Acne is one of the most complex conditions in dermatology — influenced by genetics, hormones, microbiome, and environmental stressors. At Skinmatics, we begin with a comprehensive skin analysis and history to understand the root cause of your acne before recommending a protocol.

Our approach combines pharmaceutical-grade topical agents with in-clinic modalities, ensuring each session builds on the last for compounding results.

Post-inflammatory hyperpigmentation and scarring — the marks acne leaves behind — require targeted resurfacing treatments. We employ layered techniques: chemical peels to accelerate cell turnover, radiofrequency microneedling for collagen remodelling, and laser resurfacing for deeper textural correction.

All treatments are performed exclusively by our board-certified dermatologists, never delegated, ensuring clinical precision at every visit.

Common Questions

Everything you want to know

We believe informed patients get better results. Here are the questions we hear most often about acne and scar correction.

Ask our doctors directly
How many sessions will I need to see results? −

Most patients notice improvement in skin clarity and texture within 2–3 sessions. For active acne, we typically recommend a minimum course of 4–6 sessions spaced 3–4 weeks apart. Deeper scarring may require 6–8 sessions depending on scar type and severity. We photograph your skin at every visit to track and discuss your progress.

Is the treatment painful? +

Discomfort levels vary by modality. Chemical peels produce a mild tingling sensation. RF microneedling is performed with topical anaesthetic cream applied 30 minutes prior, making the procedure well-tolerated. Laser treatments may feel like brief snapping warmth. We always prioritise your comfort and adjust intensity accordingly.

What is the downtime after treatment? +

Most of our acne treatments carry minimal downtime — mild redness for a few hours. Chemical peels may cause 2–3 days of gentle flaking. After RF microneedling, skin appears pink for 24–48 hours. Fractional laser resurfacing carries 4–7 days of social downtime. We provide detailed aftercare instructions and a tailored skincare kit post-procedure.

Are the results permanent? +

Scar correction results are long-lasting — the collagen remodelling from microneedling and laser is permanent. Active acne, however, is a chronic condition influenced by hormones and lifestyle factors. We provide guidance on maintenance protocols and home-care regimens to sustain your results and prevent future breakouts.

Is this safe for darker skin tones? +

Absolutely. Our dermatologists are highly experienced in treating Fitzpatrick types IV–VI, which are most common in South Asian populations and most susceptible to post-inflammatory hyperpigmentation. We select peels, laser wavelengths, and parameters specifically calibrated for melanin-rich skin to ensure safety and efficacy.
